About StreamFlix

A modern, Netflix-inspired web-based IPTV player that streams live TV channels from open playlists with a premium user experience.

Live Streaming

Access thousands of live TV channels from around the world. Supports HLS (.m3u8) streams with adaptive quality for smooth playback on any connection.

Smart Relay

Our intelligent backend relay system solves common streaming issues like CORS and mixed content, ensuring channels play reliably even on secure HTTPS sites.

Cross-Platform

Designed for every screen. Use it as a PWA on your phone, a desktop app on your computer, or simply in your web browser. The experience is seamless.
